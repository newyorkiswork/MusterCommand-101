Drill vs Live — why two modes matter

Mode confusion kills urgency. When people assume "it's just a drill," response slows. v3 makes the modes unmistakable: amber + 🎯 + "practice" framing for drills; pulsing red + "THIS IS NOT A DRILL" strip for live events. Toggle it above ↑
Drills get gamified, live doesn't. Drill check-in shows your time vs last drill and floor score — practice motivation. Live check-in is calm and informational only: celebration would be tone-deaf in a real emergency.
Same muscle memory. Layout, buttons, and flow are identical in both modes — drills build the exact reflexes used in a real event. Only tone, color, and copy shift.
Live mode strips distractions. In live mode the app silences all other notifications, keeps the screen awake, and boosts brightness — the phone becomes a dedicated evacuation device.
